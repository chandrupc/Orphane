How It Works

Orphane is the interface between orphanages and donar. It is a tool to help the donar to find the orphanages.

Orphanages

Orphanages will post their needs.

Donor

Donor will get the orphanages needs, and they can respond to them. They can donate the monthly fund.

Subscribers

Subscribers will get important notifications from orphanages. They can respond to them.
